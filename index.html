About Me

I am a dedicated Unity Game Developer with over four years of hands-on experience crafting engaging, high-performance games. My foundation as a certified software engineer and gold medalist equips me with both the technical and analytical capabilities required to tackle diverse development challenges. I have consistently excelled in collaborative, multi-developer environments, utilizing Git, GitLab, GitHub and Git Flow methodologies in both Spartans Global and Hazel Mobile to manage version control and ensure seamless team integration. My domain expertise spans multiple genres including FPS, hyper-casual, puzzle, car stunt, bus simulator, bike stunt and idle arcade games—demonstrating my adaptability across a wide range of gameplay experiences.

Specializing in FPS shooting mechanics and physics-based systems, I bring deep technical knowledge to the development of immersive and scalable gameplay. My portfolio includes conventional as well as performance-critical mobile games. I have a proven track record of diagnosing and resolving ANRs (Application Not Responding errors), crash reports, and runtime bottlenecks. My optimization skill set includes shader variant reduction, advanced texture compression techniques, sprite atlasing, and memory management—ensuring optimal balance between visual quality and runtime efficiency across varied hardware profiles.
